Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 3, 2025

At 3TDShop, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic account details such as name, email, and profile picture (when logging in via Facebook, Google, or direct registration).
  • Transaction data including orders, cart activity, and purchase history.
  • Technical data such as cookies, browser type, and device information for improving user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide login and account management features.
  • To process orders and manage your shopping cart.
  • To personalize your shopping experience and improve our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations and security requirements.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We only share data in the following cases:

  • With trusted service providers (such as payment processors, hosting providers) to operate our services.
  • When required by law, regulation, or court order.

4. User Rights

  • You may request to access, update, or delete your account information at any time.
  • You can withdraw your consent for data processing by contacting us.
  • For Facebook Login or Google Login users, you may revoke access via your social account settings.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve site performance and personalize content. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

6. Data Security

We apply industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.
